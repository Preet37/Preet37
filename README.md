# Preet Karia

**Robotics & Computer Engineering** · **UC Santa Cruz** · **Class of 2027**

I design and build **autonomous systems**, **perception and SLAM**, and **AI-driven software**—from thesis research and Formula-style vehicle work to **production-minded prototypes** built under hackathon deadlines. I care about systems that work in the real world: sensing, embedded stack, and clear interfaces between hardware and software.
